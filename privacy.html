Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

RoadVoice is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data and do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Data We Collect

RoadVoice accesses the following data on your device:

  • Location: Your GPS location is used to determine your current position, speed, and heading. This is required for the app to announce nearby points of interest and detect when you enter new towns.
  • Favorites: Your selected favorite brands are stored locally on your device.
  • Settings: Your app preferences are stored locally on your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect or store your location history
  • We do not track your trips or routes
  • We do not collect personal information like name, email, or phone number
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not show advertisements

Third-Party Services

RoadVoice uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim: For reverse geocoding (converting GPS coordinates to city/town names). Only your current coordinates are sent; no personal data is included.
  • Open-Elevation API: For altitude data on the map. Only your current coordinates are sent.

Data Storage

All app data (favorites, settings, custom POIs) is stored locally on your device. We do not have servers that store your data.

Children's Privacy

RoadVoice does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please email us at roadvoiceapp@gmail.com.
